Bike Prediction Tool

Using inputs of Working Day, Temperature, Humidity and Weather Conditions the bike prediction tool uses Random Forest Regression to predict the bike demand. This prediction is shown as the number of bikes demanded for any given hour of the day. The outcome accuracy will be given in terms of mean absolute error(MAE). MAE can be explained as the average amount of bikes the prediction tool is off. In order to meet the needs of the prediction tool, MAE can be added to the prediction given to better estimate the required number of bikes at any given hour of the day.
